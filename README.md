## About

I am a **Computer Science Engineering undergraduate at Bennett University**, building at the intersection of **software engineering, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and full-stack product development**.

My engineering approach goes beyond writing code. I focus on designing systems that are **scalable, maintainable, testable, user-centered, and production-ready**. From deploying software into real business environments to engineering dual-mode PWA architectures and ML-powered decision systems, I enjoy transforming complex problems into products that create measurable value.

My core interests span **software architecture, AI/ML systems, intelligent automation, full-stack engineering, distributed product systems, developer tooling, and polished human-computer experiences**. I work with a product engineering mindset, balancing technical depth with performance, usability, reliability, and real-world impact.
